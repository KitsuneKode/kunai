Kunai · redesign v2 — warm / hearth

One disciplined token set. The neutral text ramp (bright → dim) carries hierarchy; color is spent only on focus/selection (amber), ready/complete (green), and errors (red). No color is ever spent on identity — provider, audio language, and episode codes are muted metadata.

The one rule: a title wins by being bright and bold, not by having a hue. Once that holds, the title stops competing with its own episode code, and the few colors left actually mean something.

The palette

From 7 overlapping hues → 3 semantic colors + a 4-step neutral ramp.

amber

focus · selection · brand · in-progress. The fox.

green

ready · complete · playable. The reward — used sparingly so it lands.

red

real, actionable error only. Never decoration.

text → dim

title · body · metadata · unavailable. The workhorse.
