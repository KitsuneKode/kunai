Queue · live, but never jumpy

A determinate, stateful bar — the percentage counts up in lock-step with the fill (both driven by one value), so it reads as real progress, not a spinning loader. The fill is rose while downloading and settles to mint on complete, crimson on failure — so color alone tells you the state. Fixed columns + tabular numbers mean digits never wobble and rows never reflow.
